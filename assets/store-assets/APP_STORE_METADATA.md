## Full Description (both stores, max 4000 chars)

Design your perfect closet storage system right from your phone. ClosetCraft makes it easy for homeowners and DIY enthusiasts to plan custom closet organization — no design experience needed.

MEASURE YOUR SPACE
• Use your phone's camera with AR technology to measure walls in seconds
• Or enter measurements manually with intuitive sliders
• Supports feet/inches and metric units
• Works for walk-in closets, reach-in closets, and wardrobes

DESIGN WITH DRAG & DROP
• Choose from 12+ storage components: shelves, hanging rods, drawer units, shoe racks, cubbies, baskets, hook rails, jewelry trays, and more
• Drag components onto your closet wall and resize to fit
• Smart grid snapping keeps everything aligned
• Real-time dimension labels show exact measurements

CHOOSE YOUR STYLE
• Pick from 10 material finishes: white melamine, natural oak, dark walnut, maple, cherry, espresso laminate, chrome wire, and more
• Preview how different materials look in your space
• Mix components to create the perfect organization system

VISUALIZE IN 3D
• See your design come to life with the interactive 3D preview
• Rotate, zoom, and explore your closet from every angle
• Front view, top-down view, and walk-through mode
• Material colors and component details rendered in real-time

SAVE & EXPORT
• Save unlimited designs to your device
• Export professional PDF design sheets with dimensions and component lists
• Share designs with family, contractors, or friends
• Duplicate and modify existing designs

BUILT FOR HOMEOWNERS & DIY
• No design experience needed — intuitive interface guides you step by step
• Helpful tips for each component (recommended heights, spacing, etc.)
• Installation tips included in every PDF export
• Works completely offline after download

Whether you're renovating your primary bedroom walk-in closet, organizing a hallway linen closet, or maximizing a small reach-in closet, ClosetCraft helps you plan the perfect storage solution before you buy a single shelf.
