Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Summary

ClosetCraft does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your designs stay on your device.

Data We Collect

None. ClosetCraft does not collect any personal data, usage analytics, or device identifiers.

Camera Access

ClosetCraft requests camera access solely for the AR measurement feature, which uses your camera to detect walls and surfaces. Photos taken during measurement are processed on-device only and are never uploaded, stored, or shared with any server or third party.

Local Storage

Your closet designs are saved to your device's local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to ClosetCraft's developers
  • Is not synced to any cloud service
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing app data

Third-Party Services

ClosetCraft does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app works entirely offline after installation.

Advertising

ClosetCraft contains no advertisements.

Children's Privacy

ClosetCraft does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is rated for all ages and contains no objectionable content.

Changes

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: privacy@closetcraft.app
